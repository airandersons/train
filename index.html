Nursery Program

  • Early literacy and numeracy skills development
  • Social and emotional intelligence building
  • Creative expression through art and music
  • Basic Christian values and character education
  • Physical coordination and motor skills enhancement

Message From The Director

Welcome to Train Up A Child Nursery and Primary School, Najjanankumbi Campus. For over 30 years, we have been committed to providing quality education that combines academic excellence with strong Christian values.

Our motto "In God We Trust" reflects our belief that true education must address the mind, body, and spirit. We take pride in our holistic approach that nurtures each child's unique gifts while instilling discipline, moral values, and a lifelong love for learning.

At Train Up A Child, we don't just teach children - we raise future leaders who will make a positive difference in our community and nation. Our graduates stand out not just for their academic achievements, but for their character, integrity, and ability to contribute meaningfully to society.

I invite you to visit our campus and see firsthand the transformative education we offer. Our doors are always open to parents who share our vision for raising well-rounded, God-fearing children.

Bishop Peter Mutebi
Founder & Director
