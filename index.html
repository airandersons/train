Nursery Program

Our nursery program focuses on foundational learning through play and discovery. We emphasize:

  • Early literacy and numeracy skills
  • Social and emotional development
  • Creative expression through art and music
  • Basic Christian values and character building
  • Physical coordination and motor skills

Message From The Director

Welcome to Train Up A Child Nursery and Primary School, Najjanankumbi Campus. For over 15 years, we have been committed to providing quality education that combines academic excellence with strong Christian values.

Our motto "In God We Trust" reflects our belief that true education must address the mind, body, and spirit. We take pride in our holistic approach that nurtures each child's unique gifts while instilling discipline, moral values, and a love for learning.

At Train Up A Child, we don't just teach children - we raise future leaders who will make a positive difference in our community and nation. I invite you to visit our campus and see firsthand the transformative education we offer.

Mrs. Sarah K. Muwanguzi
School Director
